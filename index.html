Dungeon Crawl

  • High Concept: This game is about an explorer lost in a dungeon trying to escape.
  • Player Experience Goals:' I want the player to feel the danger and uncertainty of being lost in a dungeon.
  • Genre: The genre of this game is a top down, twin stick shooter.
  • Game play: The player is able to move round with the WASD keys and shoot with the arrow keys. The core mechanic of this game is being able to shoot at enemies. There will also be a dodge mechanic that the player can use to dodge past enemies and not take damage. The dodge mechanic will be performed using the space bar.
  • Prototype goal:\ The mechanic this game is evaluating is the shooting mechanic and the dodge mechanic.
  • Features: The dodge mechanic is not in all top down twin stick shooters so this aspect will set this game apart from the rest.
  • Setting/Premise: You are an explorer, lost in a dungeon. You do not know how you ended up in the dungeon so you must fight your way out and find clues along the way to discover why how you ended up in such a dangerous place.
  • Story: A young explorer awakes to find himself lost in the middle of a dungeon. He must fight his way out of the dungeon and possibly find clues as to how and why he ended up there.
  • Target Audience: The demographic this game is reaching out to is those interested in a mystery story with simple and concise gameplay mechanics.
  • Play time: TBD
  • Strategies: Always try and kite your enemies and do not forget to use the dodge mechanic.
