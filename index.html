Dungeon Crawl

  • High Concept: This game is about an explorer lost in a dungeon trying to escape.
  • Player Experience Goals:' I want the player to feel the eeriness and uncertainty of being lost in a dungeon.
  • Genre: The genre of this game is a top down puzzle maze.
  • Game play: The player is able to move round with the arrow keys. The core mechanic of this game is being able to move around the dungeon and push blocks.
  • Prototype goal: The mechanic this game is evaluating the puzzle mechanic of mazes and pushing blocks.
  • Features: Pushable blocks!
  • Setting/Premise: You are an explorer, lost in a dungeon. You do not know how you ended up in the dungeon so you must find your way out and discover why and how you ended up in such a strange place.
  • Story: A young explorer awakes to find himself lost in the middle of a dungeon. He must find his way out of the dungeon and possibly find clues as to how and why he ended up there.
  • Target Audience: The demographic this game is reaching out to is those interested in a mystery story with simple and concise gameplay mechanics.
